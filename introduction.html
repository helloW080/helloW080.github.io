人物介绍

          里奥·梅西(Lionel Messi)，1987年6月24日出生于阿根廷圣菲省罗萨里奥市，阿根廷足球运动员，司职前锋，现效力于巴塞罗那足球俱乐部。

          2000年，梅西加入巴塞罗那俱乐部。2005年，阿根廷青年队夺取世青赛冠军，梅西赢得了金球奖和金靴奖双项大奖。2008年北京奥运会上，梅西随阿根廷国奥队夺取了金牌。2009年底，他当选了欧洲足球先生和世界足球先生。2008-2009赛季，梅西率领球队连夺西甲、国王杯和欧冠三个冠军。2011年，梅西获得首届国际足联金球奖，还获得了欧足联欧洲最佳球员。2013年，他以46粒联赛进球的成绩第三次获得欧洲金靴奖奖杯。

          2014年，梅西随阿根廷队参加2014年巴西世界杯，最终获得世界杯亚军。2014年12月20日，梅西被IFFHS评为2013年世界职业联赛的最佳射手奖。2015年8月，当选欧洲超级杯最佳球员。2015年8月27日，获得欧洲最佳球员。2016年1月12日，梅西荣膺2015年度FIFA金球奖，五度得奖创纪录。6月，在连续三次决赛（2014年世界杯、2015年美洲杯、2016年美洲杯）失利后，梅西正式宣布将退出阿根廷国家队。8月13日，梅西正式回归阿根廷国家队。

          2017年11月24日，梅西领取了2016-17赛季的欧洲金靴奖。11月25日，巴萨官方和梅西续约到2021年。2018年9月，入选国际足联年度最佳阵容。2019年9月24日，梅西第六次当选世界足球先生。12月，梅西生涯第六次获得金球奖。





          Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987 in Rosario, Santa Fe Province, Argentina, is an Argentine football player, a striker and currently plays for FC Barcelona.

          In 2000, Messi joined the Barcelona club. In 2005, the Argentine youth team won the World Youth Championship, Messi won both the Golden Globe Award and the Golden Boot Award. At the 2008 Beijing Olympics, Messi won the gold medal with the Argentina Olympic team. At the end of 2009, he was elected as European Footballer and World Footballer. In the 2008-2009 season, Messi led the team to win three consecutive La Liga, Kings Cup and Champions League. In 2011, Messi won the first FIFA Golden Globe Award and also won UEFA's best player in Europe. In 2013, he won the European Golden Boot Award for the third time with 46 league goals.

          In 2014, Messi participated in the 2014 Brazil World Cup with the Argentina team and eventually won the World Cup runner-up. On December 20, 2014, Messi was named the best scorer of the 2013 World Professional League by IFFHS. In August 2015, was elected the best player of the European Super Cup. On August 27, 2015, he won the best player in Europe. On January 12, 2016, Messi won the 2015 FIFA Golden Globe Awards and won a record five times. In June, after losing in three consecutive finals (2014 World Cup, 2015 Copa America, 2016 Copa America), Messi officially announced that he would withdraw from the Argentina national team. On August 13, Messi officially returned to the Argentina national team.

          On November 24, 2017, Messi received the European Golden Boot Award for the 2016-17 season. On November 25, the Barcelona official and Messi renewed their contract until 2021. In September 2018, he was selected as the FIFA Team of the Year. On September 24, 2019, Messi was elected the World Footballer for the sixth time. In December, Messi won the Golden Globe for the sixth time in his career.
