俱乐部荣誉

          阿根廷巨星梅西个人第6次获得金球奖，成为历史上获得该奖项次数最多的球员。首先梅西对那些为自己投票的记者表达了感谢。“很难用言语形容现在这一刻，10年前，我获得了个人生涯中第一座金球奖杯，也是在这里——巴黎。今天，我拿到了个人第6座金球奖杯，这是我人生中特殊的一刻。”梅西在颁奖典礼上表示，“我从未放弃过梦想，我一直想要继续成长，继续进步。”

          2009年的12月1日，梅西获得了个人第一座金球奖。过去10年期间，梅西一共获得了5座金球奖，包括2009年至2012年的4连庄。如今，作为当代足坛的绝代双骄，梅西也因此超越C罗的5次，成为史上金球奖获得次数最多的球员。

          上赛季，梅西代表巴萨出战50场，贡献51球22助，巴萨获得了队史第26个西甲冠军。除此之外，他还获得了欧冠最佳射手和欧洲金靴奖等荣誉。

          “很荣幸能够成为巴萨历史的一部分，在这样一个历史最优秀之一的俱乐部踢球对我来说非常重要。我能够取得今天的成绩并不仅仅是我个人的努力，也是球队的努力。我对巴萨的情感要胜过任何纸面上的签约或者协议。”梅西表示。





          Argentine superstar Messi won the Golden Globe Award for the sixth time, becoming the player with the most awards in history. First, Messi thanked the journalists who voted for him. "It is difficult to describe in words at this moment. 10 years ago, I won the first Golden Globe trophy in my personal career, and here is Paris. Today, I got my sixth Golden Globe trophy. This is my life. A special moment. "Messi said at the awards ceremony," I have never given up my dream, I have always wanted to continue to grow and continue to improve. "

          On December 1, 2009, Messi won his first Golden Globe Award. In the past 10 years, Messi has won a total of 5 Golden Globes, including 4 consecutive villages from 2009 to 2012. Today, as the peerless arrogant of contemporary football, Messi has also surpassed Ronaldo 5 times, becoming the player with the most Golden Globe awards in history.

          Last season, Messi represented Barcelona in 50 games and contributed 51 goals and 22 assists. Barcelona won the 26th La Liga title in team history. In addition, he has won honors such as the Champions League's best shooter and the European Golden Boot Award.

          "It's an honor to be part of Barcelona's history, and it's very important for me to play in one of the best clubs in history. It is not only my personal effort but also the team's effort that I can achieve today's results. My feelings for Barcelona are better than any signing or agreement on paper, "Messi said.
