6夺金靴奖

          2019年10月16日晚，梅西和自己的家人一起出席了欧洲金靴奖的颁奖典礼，阿根廷前锋在巴塞罗那领取了个人职业生涯的第6座欧洲金靴奖杯，并再次创造历史，成为获得该奖项次数最多的球员。巧合的是，这一天也是他在正式比赛中完成巴萨一线队首秀的15周年纪念日。15年前，巴塞罗那对阵西班牙人，比赛进行到82分钟，身披30号球衣的梅西替补上场，职业生涯首次代表巴萨出战正式比赛，那时的他才只有17岁114天。

          梅西在颁奖仪式上表示，“欧冠有其特殊之处，每年球队都想赢得冠军。但最重要的是西甲联赛，我们永远不会忘记联赛和杯赛，球队想赢得一切。”此外，梅西感谢了自己的家人、教练和队友，对来到现场的阿尔巴和苏亚雷斯也表示感激。梅西表示：“没有他们，我今天就不能站在这里领奖，甚至有可能一座金靴奖也拿不到。”

          上赛季，梅西代表巴萨在西甲联赛共打进36球。欧冠联赛梅西出场10次，打入12粒进球，成为西甲欧冠双料射手王。

          此前，梅西分别在2009-2010赛季(34球)、2011-2012赛季(50球)、2012-2013赛季(46球)、2016-2017赛季(37球)和2017-2018赛季(34球)5次夺得过欧洲金靴奖。本届再次摘靴，梅西在这一奖项的得奖数已经领先C罗两次。同时，梅西也成为了欧洲金靴积分制以来第一位完成欧洲金靴奖3连庄的球员。





          On the evening of October 16, 2019, Messi and his family attended the award ceremony of the European Golden Boot Award. The Argentine striker received the sixth European Golden Boot trophy in his career in Barcelona, and once again made history and became a winner. The player with the most awards. Coincidentally, this day is also the 15th anniversary of the completion of the Barcelona first team debut in the official game. Fifteen years ago, Barcelona played against the Spaniards. The game lasted 82 minutes. Messi, wearing a No. 30 jersey, came on the bench. For the first time in his professional career, he played for Barcelona. At that time, he was only 17 years and 114 days.

          Messi said at the awarding ceremony, "The Champions League has something special. Every year the team wants to win the championship. But the most important thing is the La Liga. We will never forget the league and the cup. The team wants to win everything." In addition, Messi thanked his family, coach and teammates, and also expressed gratitude to Alba and Suarez who came to the scene. Lionel Messi said: "Without them, I would not be able to stand here today to receive the award, and maybe even a golden boot award could not be won."

          Last season, Messi scored 36 goals in the La Liga on behalf of Barcelona. UEFA Champions League Messi made 10 appearances and scored 12 goals to become the La Liga Champions League double shooter king.

          Previously, Messi was in the 2009-2010 season (34 goals), 2011-2012 season (50 goals), 2012-2013 season (46 goals), 2016-2017 season (37 goals) and 2017-2018 season (34 goals). Won the European Golden Boot Award 5 times. Picking boots again this time, Messi has won the award twice ahead of Ronaldo. At the same time, Messi has also become the first player to complete the European Golden Boot Award 3 consecutive games since the European Golden Boot Points System.
